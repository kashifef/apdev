Top-load Agitator Washers

Washers with impellers are designed to move the laundry to the bottom to do the washing. An impeller is more gentle on clothes compared to an agitator.

Wifi-Compatiable Washers & Dryers

Simplify your life by connecting your laundry units to your mobile phone. This will allow you to check the progress of your wash and receive an alert when your cycle has finished.

Top-load Impellar Washers

An Agitator creates motion by shaking and forcing water through the machine. If you need more aggressive or more powerful cleaning for very dirty clothes, this is best suited for you.

Steam Option

Unlike water and even hot water, steam is hotter and can penetrate and open the very smallest pores of clothing. This works as a kind of prewash that is more effective than soaking.

Dryers with Stainless Steel Drums

A stainless steel drum will never rust, it can resist scratches from metal on clothing and it can even avoid being dented by sneakers and other things that people put in their dryers.
